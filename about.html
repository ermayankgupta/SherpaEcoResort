Revive your Body and Soul at our Eco Friendly Resort

Sherpa Eco Resort is a 3 hour drive from city of Chandigarh on the Shimla highway and is conveniently located just 14 km from the city of Solan in Himachal Pradesh. It’s a mountain getaway amidst pristine forests of Oak and Pine at about 5500 feet above mean sea level. The breathtaking panoramic view of the open valley right from the window of the cottage is just mesmerizing. The exhilarating off road experience is bound to pump up the Adrenalin of any adventure lover. It’s a complete holiday destination to rejuvenate your spirits and enrich your memories.

The unique blend of Agriculture and Tourism activity here makes it Himachal’s first tourism Project where you can enjoy Natural Farm Fresh Vegetables from fields and Polyhouses, Fresh Seasonal Fruits, Milk from home grown cows and fresh seasonal fruits, Natural Spring Water and lot more. You can also learn about Agriculture by working at our farms and give back something to Mother Earth.

The project embodies principles of Sustainable Ecotourism to minimize the environmental impacts of tourist activity in this virgin ecosystem. and We work with the local community to preserve rich bio diversity of this unique place which is home to diverse wildlife like Leopard, Barking Deer, Jungle Fowl, Exotic Birds and Butter flies.

Nestled away from the maddening crowd of popular tourist destinations, we promise the real experience of solitude & wilderness which is hallmark of holidays in the mountains.

The promoters are two immensely passionate & talented professionals, Mr. Vinay Tuli and Mr. Umesh Mahajan who have academic backgrounds in Horticulture & Tourism Management and a rich experience of working in corporate and social sectors for almost 25 years.
